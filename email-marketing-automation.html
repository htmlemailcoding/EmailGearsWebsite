Our Services

What’s Covered?

Journeys & Email Sequences

Whether you're looking to engage customers with a drip campaign or drive revenue through a multi-step email sequence, we have the perfect solution for you.

Welcome Program

Introduce your brand and build lasting relationships with new subscribers. EmailGears can help you create an engaging welcome program that captures the attention and trust of your prospects.

Re-Engagement and Winback Programs

Bring inactive subscribers and customers back to life with captivating and relevant emails. EmailGears can help you rekindle interest and get disengaged subscribers to open, read, and interact with your emails again.

Browse Abandon Program

Turn website visitors into buyers with automated email campaigns triggered by their browsing behavior. EmailGears helps you recover potential sales from those who are still exploring your site.

Cart Abandon Program

Cart abandonment is a major source of lost revenue. With EmailGears’ cart abandonment automation, we can help you recover lost sales and boost your conversion rates.

Next Logical Product Program

Automated emails that recommend products related to recent purchases can significantly boost your revenue. For instance, when a customer buys a pair of running shoes, an automated email suggesting athletic gear, socks, or fitness accessories can help increase their purchase value.
