Our Services

What’s Covered?

Dedicated Account Manager

At EmailGears, each client is paired with a Dedicated Account Manager who oversees the daily operations of your email marketing program. Your Account Manager will be your primary contact, ensuring smooth coordination between you and our team to execute your campaign needs effectively.

Email Deployment

We’ll handle the setup in your chosen ESP platform, including email campaigns, audience segments, and sending the emails. Let us take care of the technical details, allowing you to focus on strategy and other initiatives.

A/B Testing and Reporting

Effective email marketing thrives on data and testing. We’ll manage your A/B testing process and provide insightful reports on the results to help refine your strategy.

Content Coordination

From the outset, we collaborate with your team to develop a 90-day content calendar that aligns with your email marketing goals and ensures your campaigns are strategically scheduled.
