Our Services

An ESP-Agnostic Experts to Help You
Find the Right ESP for Your Business

Initial Audit

We begin with a preliminary audit to identify the challenges you’re facing with your current ESP. This could include missing reports, blocked integrations, data issues, or any other roadblocks that are affecting your email marketing.

Vendor Research and Shortlisting

After pinpointing the problems, we research and shortlist the ESPs that can meet your requirements. With hundreds of vendors in the market, we identify 3 platforms that best match your needs.

Live ESP Demos

We then coordinate with the shortlisted ESPs to schedule demos. These demos allow you to objectively assess each platform and make an informed decision about which one is the best fit.

Honest & Unbiased Advice

We do not resell any platforms, so you can trust that our recommendations are honest and unbiased. We’ll always provide you with the best, most objective advice for selecting a vendor.

No Commissions. Full Transparency.

As a vendor-agnostic agency, we don’t receive commissions from any ESP. Any discounts or commission benefits we receive are passed directly to you, ensuring our process always works in your best interest.
