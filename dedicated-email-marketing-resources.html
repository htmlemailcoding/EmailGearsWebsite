Our Services

What’s Covered?

Dedicated Account Managers

Your main point of contact throughout our partnership. Your Account Managers will oversee the allocation of hours across the team for your campaigns and initiatives, ensuring smooth day-to-day email operations.

Email Campaign Managers

This expert will handle the creation, testing, and deployment of your campaigns within your email platform. They’ll also focus on performance optimization and reporting. Our campaign managers are experienced with over 40 ESPs, including Iterable, Braze, Cordial, SFMC, and others.

Email Designers and Developers

Our designers and developers create email designs that drive ROI and ensure high-quality code. These emails are thoroughly tested across 40+ email clients, browsers, and operating systems to provide a consistent user experience.

Email Marketing Strategists

You’ll have access to a team of strategists who will craft email marketing strategies tailored to your business. They will assist with broadcast campaign strategy, audience segmentation, testing and optimization plans, channel integration, and more.

Email Platform Experts

Our certified ESP specialists can support complex programs, helping you build automations like welcome programs, cart abandonment sequences, upsell/cross-sell campaigns, nurture sequences, and more.

Email Copywriters

If you need copywriting services, our team of skilled copywriters will write, edit, and optimize content for both your email marketing and SMS campaigns.
