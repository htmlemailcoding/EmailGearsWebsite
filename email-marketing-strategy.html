Our Services

What’s Covered?

Email Marketing Audit

We provide an in-depth review of your entire email marketing program. We’ll identify what’s working, what’s not, and offer guidance on where to make improvements.

Email Testing and Optimization Plans

Successful email marketing relies on ongoing testing and optimization. We can help you create and implement testing strategies that will deliver measurable results for your business.

Email Automation and Campaign Strategy

There’s no one-size-fits-all approach here. We’ll develop a tailored email strategy that aligns with your business, your sales cycle, and your customers, covering both automated email sequences and manual broadcast messages.

List Growth and Nurturing Strategy

Your business is unique, and so is your email list. We’ll assist you in building and nurturing your list with effective strategies that turn subscribers into paying customers.

Channel Integration Strategy

We can also help you expand into SMS, push notifications, and in-app messaging. Together, we’ll create a cross-channel strategy to ensure your message reaches the right audience through the right platform.

Email Vendor Evaluation

The email service provider (ESP) you use can greatly influence the success of your email marketing strategy. If you need advice or support in selecting the best platform, we’re here to help.
